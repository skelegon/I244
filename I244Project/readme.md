    <h2>About this webpage</h2>
      <div>
        <p>This webpage is a platform for trading items with other users.</p>
        <p>In a nutshell, users can upload items they wish to trade and browse items by other users. Having found a desireable item, user can send a trade proposal to the other party, proposing an item from their own list as a trade. The request is then sent to the other party who can, after reviewing the proposal, either accept or decline it. Should the party accept the trade, such notification will be sent to the other party with an email address and a phone number to contact the other party to complete the trade.</p>
        <p>This webpage is created as a school project in subject "Network Applications I: Client-Server Systems" in Estonian Information Technology College in 2016.</p>
        <p>The project looks to implement main elements and technologies of the modern applications, including:</p>
          <ul>
            <li>HTML ja CSS for the framework and design of the webpage;</li>
            <li>JavaScript to add interactivity;</li>
            <li>PHP to retrieve and process information from the web-server;</li>
            <li>MySQL for database applications;</li>
          </ul>
        <p>The webpage utilises Bootstrap - a popular HTML, CSS, and JS framework for developing responsive projects.</p>
      </div>

    <h2>How to use this site?</h2>
    <div>
      <h4>Regular user</h4>
      <p>To start using the site, first register as a user. Option to sign up can be found from the "Login" tab at the top right of the page.</p>
      <p>Once registered, sign in from the sign from the "Login" tab.</p>
      <p>Having signed in, menu button "Trade" and drop-down list "My-stuff" appear on the left side of the navigation bar.</p>
      <p>From "Trade" menu, you can view items other users wish to trade. You can browse the items by category using the list of categories on the left side on the "Trade" view</p>
      <p>Clicking on a product will take you to the product window where you can see more details about the item and propose a trade with one of your own items to the item owner.</p>
      <p>Under "My stuff" there are the following menu items:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><b>My products</b> - List of the products you have uploaded;</li>
        <li><b>New item</b> - Add a new product to be display on the site and trade;</li>
        <li><b>Trade requests</b> - view and cancel the requests you have sent to other users and view and accept or cancel incoming requests from other users;</li>
        <li><b>Notifications</b> - view the requests that have a response from the other party, and remove notifications you do not need anymore.</li>
      </ul>
      <p>To sign out, press the "Logout" button on the top right of the page.</p><br>

      <h4>As an admin</h4>
      <p>Admin has a couple of more features available compared to a regular user:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><b>Users</b> - View users and suspend or un-suspend users from using the site;</li>
        <li><b>Items</b> - View the list of items of all the users and suspend and un-suspend certain items;</li>
        <li><b style="color: salmon">!! Reset initial state !!</b> - For demo purposes only - to reset website to initial state;</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
